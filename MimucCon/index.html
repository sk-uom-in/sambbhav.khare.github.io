Manchester Interdisciplinary Mathematics Undergraduate Conference (MIMUC)

The First ever Manchester Interdisciplinary Mathematics Undergraduate Conference will take place in April 2022. Aiming to improve the learning experience of undergraduate students of the University of Manchester and to encourage interdisciplinary research and collaboration across students of STEM and offer them the opportunity to develop skills that are not taught in a lecture theatre. Some of the Topics for discussion are:-

  • Pure Mathematics
  • Applied Mathematics and Theoretical Physics
  • Computational Mathematics and Maths in Computer Science
  • Data Science and Machine Learning
  • Theoretical and Applied Mechanics
  • Statistics
  • Operational Research
  • Financial Mathematics
  • Actuarial Mathematics
  • Biological Mathematics
  • Mathematics in Education
